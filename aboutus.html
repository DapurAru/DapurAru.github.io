ABOUT US

SAHABAT TANI



PT Sahabat Tani is a company engaged in coconut processing, established in 2022 and located in West Java Province. The company has a vision to become a leading player in the coconut processing industry by providing high-quality products for both domestic and international markets. In its effort to expand its business reach, PT Sahabat Tani has established two subsidiaries located in Payakumbuh City, West Sumatra. The two subsidiaries are named PT Palokoto Agro Industri, which focuses on the processing and distribution of coconut derivative products. Supported by a strong production network, PT Sahabat Tani and its subsidiaries are committed to continuously innovating and contributing positively to the development of the agricultural and coconut industries in Indonesia.

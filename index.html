ABOUT US

SAHABAT TANI

PT Sahabat Tani is a company engaged in coconut processing, established in 2022 and located in West Java Province.

The company has a vision to become a leading player in the coconut processing industry by providing high-quality products for both domestic and international markets. In its effort to expand its business reach, PT Sahabat Tani has established two subsidiaries located in Payakumbuh City, West Sumatra.

The two subsidiaries are named PT Palokoto Agro Industri, which focuses on the processing and distribution of coconut derivative products. Supported by a strong production network, PT Sahabat Tani and its subsidiaries are committed to continuously innovating and contributing positively to the development of the agricultural and coconut industries in Indonesia.

WE ARE SUPPLIER AND SPECIALIST EXPORTER OF COCONUT PRODUCTS

Sahabat Tani is a leading coconut supplier from Indonesia, boasting generations of expertise in developing coconut-based products. We strive for a sustainable synergy that harmonizes business with environmental considerations, prioritizing social stability within our communities. Fueled by a spirit of innovation, we focus on research to create products that are vital to our partners and consumers.

Social Inclusion

SAHABAT TANI focuses on empowering local communities through programs that support coconut farmers and the surrounding communities. We believe that the active involvement of all stakeholders, including farmers, their families, and the general community, is key to creating a sustainable economy. Through skills training, access to markets, and technological support, SAHABAT TANI is committed to improving the quality of life for coconut farmers while ensuring that their production is fairly valued. By integrating the principles of social inclusion into every aspect of our operations, we aim to create stronger, self-reliant communities where everyone has the opportunity to contribute and benefit from the economic growth generated.
